**Explain your experience with EC2, S3, IAM, and VPC.**


EC2

I managed Linux-based EC2 instances running production applications. My responsibilities included provisioning instances, troubleshooting performance issues, managing EBS volumes, creating AMIs, performing patching, and working with Auto Scaling Groups.

S3

I used S3 for application backups, storing logs, and artifacts. I configured lifecycle policies for cost optimization and managed bucket permissions using IAM and bucket policies.

IAM

I created and managed IAM users, roles, and policies following the principle of least privilege. I also configured IAM roles for EC2 and Kubernetes workloads to securely access AWS services without storing credentials.

VPC

I have hands-on experience with VPC networking, including configuring public and private subnets, route tables, Internet Gateways, NAT Gateways, Security Groups, and Network ACLs. I have also troubleshot networking and connectivity issues between applications.
