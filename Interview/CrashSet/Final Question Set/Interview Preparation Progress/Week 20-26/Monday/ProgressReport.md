## Key Concepts To Reinforce

- Do not jump directly to commands.
- Start with:
  1. Understand impact
  2. Check monitoring
  3. Identify affected layer
  4. Collect evidence
  5. Mitigate issue
  6. RCA and prevention

- Explain:
  - Why you check a metric
  - Why you check a log
  - Why you choose a particular command
