# My Role in Mastercard Project

In the Mastercard project, I worked as a **Site Reliability Engineer**, where my primary responsibility was to ensure production reliability and availability of the payment platform.

I was involved in production monitoring, deployment support, P1/P2 incident handling, root cause analysis, certificate management, and automation of repetitive operational tasks using scripting.

I worked with monitoring tools like Splunk and Dynatrace to troubleshoot application issues, analyze logs, and identify the root cause of production incidents.

My overall goal was to maintain a stable, secure, and highly available production environment while reducing downtime and improving reliability.
