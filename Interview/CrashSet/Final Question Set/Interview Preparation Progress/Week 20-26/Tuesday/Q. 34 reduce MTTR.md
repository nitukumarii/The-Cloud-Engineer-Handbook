# How do you reduce MTTR?

To reduce MTTR, I focus on faster detection, efficient troubleshooting, and preventing repeat incidents.

- Use proper monitoring and alerting to detect issues quickly.
- Maintain clear runbooks and troubleshooting steps.
- Use logs, metrics, and traces to identify the root cause faster.
- Automate repetitive recovery tasks where possible.
- Ensure proper incident communication and collaboration between teams.
- Perform RCA after incidents and implement preventive actions.
