# How do you ensure recurring incidents are permanently resolved?

To prevent recurring incidents, I perform a detailed Root Cause Analysis (RCA) after the incident.

Based on the RCA findings, I implement permanent fixes such as improving configurations, automating repetitive tasks, and adding better monitoring or alerts.

I continue observing the system behavior through monitoring dashboards to ensure the issue does not happen again.

I also document the solution in runbooks and update the process to improve future incident handling.
