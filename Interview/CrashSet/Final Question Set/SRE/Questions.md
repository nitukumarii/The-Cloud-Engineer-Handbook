# 🚀 SRE Interview Questions

1. What are SLIs, SLOs, and SLAs?

2. What is an error budget and how is it used?

3. How do you reduce MTTR (Mean Time To Recovery)?

4. Describe a time when you improved system reliability or reduced MTTR.

5. Explain the RCA (Root Cause Analysis) process you follow after a production incident.

6. Describe a production incident that taught you an important lesson.

7. Describe a major production incident you handled.

8. Tell us about your most challenging production issue.

9. Explain a recent P1/Sev1 incident you handled.

10. How do you handle multiple P1 incidents occurring at the same time?

11. Describe a situation where you had to troubleshoot with incomplete information.

12. Describe a situation where the infrastructure was healthy but the application was failing. How did you identify the root cause?

13. Describe a situation where Splunk, Dynatrace, CloudWatch, Prometheus, or Grafana helped you identify the root cause.

14. Tell me about a time when you automated a repetitive operational task.

15. Describe a time when you learned a new technology quickly and successfully implemented it.

16. Tell me about a project where you went above and beyond your regular responsibilities.

17. Tell me about a time when you went above and beyond for a customer.

18. Have you ever worked in an on-call support rotation?

19. Do you enjoy being on-call?

20. Day-to-day Linux performance troubleshooting — What is your general troubleshooting approach?

21. Application is slow—how do you investigate?

22. How do you troubleshoot an application outage?

23. How do you handle production deployments?

24. How do you validate after patch?

25. Describe a deployment failure you handled.

26. Describe a rollback you performed after a failed deployment.
